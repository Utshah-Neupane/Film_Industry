     Chakka Panja is all set for UAE screenings.

         This year’s runaway hit Chhakka Panja which was released on 30th September is all set to travel to the United Arab Emirates (UAE), a Gulf country that has emerged as a major hub for Nepali migrants. The Deepashree Niraula directorial,that broke all previous records in Nepali box office earlier this month, is now to be screened at 17 film theatres across the UAE.

        The screening of Chhakka Panja in the UAE is also noteworthy because of the theme it represents is the impact of foreign employment on Nepali families. It achieved an enourmous success at home, collecting more than Rs 12 crores, the highest ever for a Nepali movie, Chhakka Panja is Deepashree Niraula’s debut project. The film features actors Deepak Raj Giri, Priyanka Karki, Shiva Hari Poudel, Jitu Nepal and Kedar Ghimire in lead roles.
