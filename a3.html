Anmol KC becomes the highest paid actor of Nepal till the date.

                Anmol KC roped in the new project KRI which will be directed by Surendra Poudel. He being signed in the project with whopping amount $18,690 (20 lacs Nrs). Subas Entertainment and Super Kajol Films team up for this love action drama. The movie will be on floor from Falgun and will be released on 2074.



              It’s official, Anmol KC has become the highest paid actor in the Nepalese film industry. He has been roped in for a new project titled ‘Cri’, meaning ‘revenge’ in Sanskrit’, by producer Subash Giri of ‘November Rain’ and ‘Classic’ fame. The ‘Jerryy’ star has been confirmed to get a whopping Rs. 2 million for the film that will be made under the banners of Subash Entertainment and Super Kajol. A new actress will be cast opposite Anmol in the film to be directed by ‘Aavash’ director Surendra Poudel. The film is scheduled to go on floor in February next year.

             Anmol KC had officially debuted as a lead actor in 2013 blockbuster film ‘Hostel’ and the rest is history. After making a hattrick with three back to back hits – ‘Hostel’, ‘Jerryy’ and ‘Dreams’; his latest film ‘Gajalu’ too has been doing wonders at the box office. Looks like he is unstoppable.
