Recent years of the Film Industry

The way movies are made and how they're approached is completely different now than it was six decades ago. Earlier only romantic movies were given priority and the movies which were made also didn't have much powerful and animated scenes whereas nowadays movies with much-more advanced technology and sophisticated design and performances are released which later results as the king in box-office.

Since 2000 AD, the film industry started becoming more and more successful in terms of critics as well as box office collection. The documentary film also rose as a commercial genre for perhaps the first time. Home theatre systems became increasingly sophisticated.Nowadays i.e since 2000 AD, Movies started becoming more sophisticated, complex as well as full of entertainment and dynamism.

There has been an increasing globalization of cinema during this decade, with foreign-language films gaining popularity in English-speaking markets. Examples of such films include Crouching Tiger, Hidden Dragon (Mandarin), Amélie (French), Lagaan (Hindi), Spirited Away (Japanese), City of God (Portuguese), The Passion of the Christ (Aramaic), Apocalypto (Mayan), Slumdog Millionaire (parts in Hindi), and Inglourious Basterds (multiple European languages).

Recently there has been a revival in 3D film popularity. 3D films have gained increasing popularity with the best critical and financial successes being in the field of feature film animation. As of 2010, the largest film industries by number of feature films produced are those of India, the United States, China, Nigeria and Japan. Beginning in 2008 with Iron Man and The Dark Knight, superhero films have greatly increased in popularity and financial success, with films based on Marvel and DC comics regularly being released every year up to the present.
