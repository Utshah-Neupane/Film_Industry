       In the film industry superstars are those high-profile successful people who have been able to rule millions of heart and box-office with their talents and abilities. Recently, we have seen many superstars who can just not be underestimeted and whose talents just can't be denied. They with the help of their personality and acting talents have been capable to grab title "The Superstar". We have seen many faces in this industry but some of them are unforgettable.
    Here is the list of some superstars in the world who are admired for their potentiality till the date.

1.Leonardo DiCaprio

Since the 2000s, DiCaprio has been praised for his work in a wide range of film genres. His succeeding films including The Man in the Iron Mask (1998), the biographical crime drama Catch Me If You Can (2002) and the epic historical drama Gangs of New York (2002) are one of the most biggest films ever released. Only 37 years old and every single one of his movies have been a hit! Starting his career from the movie "This Boy's Life" to the greatest movie of 2010 "INCEPTION", his career has been something every actor wishes for. Most people remember him for his breathtaking performance as Jack in Titanic.

2. Brad Pitt

An actor and producer known as much for his versatility as he is for his handsome face, Golden Globe-winner Brad Pitt's most widely recognized role may be Tyler Durden in Fight Club. However, his performances of Billy Beane in Moneyball, and Rusty Ryan in the remake of Ocean's Eleven and its sequels, are also admirable. This man has went from fighting in clubs to aging backwards. It's surprising that he hasn't won an Oscar yet!

3.Bhuwan K.C

Bhuwan K.C (born: 23 August 1957) is a Nepali film actor, producer, and director. Starting his career as a singer for Radio Nepal with hits such as "Jaage Saara Raat", he went on to become a superstar of the Nepali film industry acting in more than a hundred films. The 1983 film Juni was his first film. Samjhana was his first hit, in 1984, and it helped to establish him as a successful actor in Nepali cinema. His other successful movies are Nepali Babu, Karodpati , Kusume Rumal, Saino, Dakshina, Kanyadan, and Chino.

4.Hari Bansha Acharya & Madan Krishna Shrestha

Hari Bansha Acharya and Madan Krishna Shrestha are the Nepalese actors and comedians.They are one of the most successful and respected comedians in the Nepalese entertainment industry.They are known for his method acting. They both make up the comedy duo MaHa Jodi. Whenever they come together in any movie, some extra interest and shine can be felt in the movie. They have won many awards for their dedication and hardwork.

5.Amitabh Bachhan

Indian Cinema today is personified with the ‘Angry Young Man’, globally. The popular actor of the 70’s had all the elements of a successful actor with the aggressive roles he performed in movies like Sholay, Zanjeer, Deewar and Agneepath, his romantic-comedy's such as Amar Akbar Anthony, Silsila and Kabhi Kabhie. Even today he is considered one of the most versatile and influential actor in the indian cinema.
