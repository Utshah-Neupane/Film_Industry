Introduction to film industry

            Film industry is the entertainment industries involved in producing and distributing movies. It is the association of the people or companies engaged in a particular kind of commercial enterprise. The film industry or motion picture industry comprises the technological and commercial institutions of filmmaking, i.e., film production companies, film studios, cinematography, film production, screenwriting, pre-production, post production, film festivals, distribution; and actors, film directors and other film crew personnel.

In today's modern world the most exotic industry serving the whole world with entertainment is the film industry. Nowadays each and every individual ranging from children to aged people prefer movies for recreation and fun. Through movies people get to know exciting information, beautiful lessons and they get opportunities for refreshment. So, this industry is given top most priority by every individual and every youths are passionate or eager to enter into this field to get name, fame, love and wealth.
